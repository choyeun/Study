# A [[conditional proposition]]

"If p then q", "p only if q"
In symbols: p -> q
[[Truth table]]

| p   | q   | p -> q |
| --- | --- | ------ |
| T   | T   | T      |
| T   | F   | F      |
| F   | T   | T      |
| F   | F   | T      |

p: antecedent or hypothesis (가정)
- [[sufficient conclusion]] (충분 조건)
q: consequent or conclusion (결론)
- [[necessary condition]] (필요조건)
